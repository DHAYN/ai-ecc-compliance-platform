FAKE CORP — WIRELESS NETWORK ACCESS POLICY
(Synthetic sample document — for PoC/demo purposes only. Not a real organizational policy.)

1. Purpose
This policy defines the minimum security requirements for wireless network
access within Fake Corp offices.

2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors connecting to any
Fake Corp wireless access point.

3. Requirements
3.1 All corporate wireless access points must use WPA2 encryption at a
    minimum. Open or unencrypted networks are not permitted.
3.2 Visitors and third parties must connect only to the designated
    Guest Network, which is logically separated from the internal
    corporate network.
3.3 Wireless access point administrative credentials must not be shared
    outside the IT department.
3.4 New access points must be approved by the IT Security team before
    being deployed.

4. Exceptions
Any exception to this policy must be requested in writing and approved
by the IT Security lead.

5. Enforcement
Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and
including termination of employment or contract.
